About Us

Overlooking Dasve Lake, these laid-back apartments are 4 minutes' walk from Lavasa Convention Centre & 30 km from Panshet Dam.
Featuring air-conditioning & mountain or lake views, the bright studios and 1- and 2-bedroom apartments offer free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs with cable channels. They also have desks. The 1-and 2-bedroom units have sofabeds, while 2-bedroom units add terraces with lake views. Bike hire is free. There's also a business centre & pets are welcome.
